What is Cryptography?

Cryptography is the practice of securing communication and information through the use of codes and encryption. It ensures that data is protected from unauthorized access and can only be decrypted by someone with the correct key.

What is Steganography?

Steganography is the art of hiding messages or information within other non-secret text or data. It is commonly used to embed information in images, audio files, or even video files in a way that it is undetectable to the human eye.
